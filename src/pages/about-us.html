About EasyHome

Revolutionize Your HousingExperience

Easy Home is your trusted partner in finding and securing housing in Nigeria. Our platform is designed to connect homeowners and tenants directly, offering transparency, affordability, and convenience in the housing market.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the housing market in Nigeria by providing a seamless, transparent, and direct platform that connects homeowners and tenants. Easy Home is dedicated to eliminating exploitation, reducing costs, and empowering users with real-time, verified information to make informed housing decisions.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's most trusted and innovative housing platform, creating a future where finding a home is effortless, transparent, and accessible to all. Easy Home envisions a housing market free from middlemen exploitation, where individuals and families find homes that meet their needs with confidence and convenience.

Our Goals

Eliminate Exploitations

We aim to eradicate the exploitation commonly faced by tenants in the Nigerian housing market. By cutting out agents and providing a direct link between homeowners and tenants, we ensure fair pricing and transparent interactions.

Enhance Accessibility

Finding a home should be simple and stress-free. Our platform is designed with user-friendly features and comprehensive search filters, enabling users to locate properties based on their preferences, such as price range, location, and amenities, all in real time.

Promote Transparency

Trust is paramount in the housing market. Through our verified property listings and Know Your Customer (KYC) processes, we ensure all users have access to accurate and reliable information, reducing the risks of fraud and misinformation.

Support Shared Living

With the rising demand for affordable housing, we facilitate shared living arrangements. Our platform connects individuals looking for roommates or shared apartments, fostering collaboration and making housing more affordable for everyone.

Foster Convenience

We prioritize convenience with features like virtual house tours that allow users to explore properties online. Additionally, our escrow services provide secure payment options, and appointment booking streamlines the process of in-person visits, saving users time and effort.
